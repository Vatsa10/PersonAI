# PersonAI

An intelligent AI assistant designed to seamlessly connect with Google Workspace services including Gmail, Calendar, and Tasks. This assistant streamlines your daily digital workflow by automating key tasks and enabling efficient management of your communications, schedules, and to-dos.

## Key Features

- **Gmail Integration**: Effortlessly compose emails, search through your Gmail messages, and manage your drafts.
- **Calendar Management**: Create new events, view and organize your schedules, and manage multiple calendars with ease.
- **Task Management**: Seamlessly create, organize, and track your Google Tasks for improved productivity.
